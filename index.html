about

after months of planning, overthinking, and a considerable amount of late night music dives, it's finally time for the little blog to be live :]

think of this as a refined version of the notes app. a space for documenting stuff, projects, shiny things, or whatever else has my attention. little snapshots of my mind, organized enough to make sense of later.

some posts are quick reflections, others projects updates, others observations i don't want to forget. all of it lives here, ready for me to revisit or you to stumble across.

read what you like, skim what you don't... this space is for me first, second for anyone else.
